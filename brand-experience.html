Phygital Integration

We merge the digital and physical realms through our phygital approach. By utilizing NFC labels embedded in physical products, we attach digital experiences or products to physical offerings, enhancing the overall customer journey. This integration occurs either during the pre-checkout stage, providing personalized recommendations and styling options, or post-purchase, offering additional digital content or experiences.
